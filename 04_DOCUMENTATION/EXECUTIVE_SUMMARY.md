## What Is BruceOps?

BruceOps is a production-ready system that enables:

1. **Daily Life Tracking** (LifeOps)
   - Energy, stress, mood, focus, sleep metrics
   - Habit tracking (exercise, substance use, etc.)
   - Daily reflections and win/friction capture
   - Automatic pattern detection

2. **Goal Management & Accountability**
   - Create goals in domains (Faith, Family, Health, Work)
   - Daily/weekly check-ins
   - Automatic completion rate calculation
   - Drift flag detection when you fall off track

3. **Idea Capture & Analysis** (ThinkOps)
   - Quick URL clipping ("save this")
   - Full idea templates with scoring
   - AI reality checks on feasibility
   - Status tracking (exploring/building/archived)

4. **AI-Powered Insights**
   - Semantic search across all your data
   - Pattern/correlation discovery
   - Multi-perspective analysis
   - Weekly narratives and synthesis

5. **Claude Desktop Integration**
   - Natural language access to all features
   - 13 purpose-built tools
   - No coding required for users
   - Seamless workflow
